Data Collection Infrastructure: Trial Implementation

🧪 Multi-Paradigm Trial Processes

Three distinct trial processes optimized for Motor Imagery, P300 Visual, and Speech Decoding paradigms, each reflecting neurophysiological requirements and experimental protocols for systematic neural signature capture.

Motor Imagery Trials

Audio-cued paradigm for capturing neural signals associated with imagined movement without actual motor execution.
Three classes: left hand, right hand, bilateral feet
Audio cue guidance with configurable timing
Rest periods for baseline data collection
Continuous EEG recording throughout phases

P300 Visual Trials

Event-related potential paradigm using visual word presentation to elicit P300 responses through focused attention.
Color word stimuli: green, purple, yellow, red, blue
Randomized presentation to prevent habituation
Configurable display and rest durations
Real-time EEG monitoring capabilities

Speech Decoding (Legacy)

Five-stage protocol exploring speech production, motor coordination, and cognitive speech processes.
Vocal expression and non-vocal thinking stages
Motor action components with/without speech
Microphone recording synchronization
15-second standardized stage durations
