Modular design separating data models, business logic, and presentation layers ensures maintainability while enabling independent development and testing of different system components. The layered architecture implements sophisticated separation of concerns with models defining data structures, views handling business logic and user interactions, and comprehensive integration enabling scalable BCI research workflows.
