Hybrid Prediction Integration

The communicator implements sophisticated hybrid prediction capabilities that combine Motor Imagery and P300 paradigms within unified interaction workflows with advanced coordination between different prediction modalities.

Motor Imagery Paradigm
Continuous control through imagined motor movements with spatial pattern recognition.
  • Continuous control capabilities
  • Spatial pattern recognition
  • Self-paced interaction
  • High information transfer rate
P300 Paradigm
Event-related potential detection for discrete selection with high accuracy classification.
  • High accuracy classification
  • Discrete selection capability
  • Robust signal detection
  • Reliable performance
