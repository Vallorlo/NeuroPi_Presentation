Traditional Multi-Stage Trials

Full-screen word presentation system with precise timing control
Configurable timing and randomization for optimal ERP elicitation
Immersive interface optimized for event-related potential research

Motor Imagery Integration

Seamless connection to BCI classification systems
Left hand, right hand, and feet imagery classes with visual cues
Real-time feedback during experiments with confidence indicators
Adaptive trial difficulty based on classification performance
